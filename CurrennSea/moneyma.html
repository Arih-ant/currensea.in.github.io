Level 1: How to start managing your money?
Basics of Stock market

First steps to Financial Freedom!!!

Divide your income into the following
  1. Needs : Basic necessities (food, rent, debt, insurance,etc.)
  2. Savings: Try to save atleast 10-20% of your income
  3. Wants : Leisure activities

Why save?
  1. Savings can help you prepare for unforeseen circumstances
  2. Because you can invest a large portion of what you have saved and be financially free
  3. You can also save for a goal like buying your dream house/ your dream car

Stay away from bad debt
  1. Bad debt can push you towards bankruptcy
  2. It is negative cashflow, meaning it constantly draws money out of your house
  3. Paying off EMIs and especially credit cards dues on time is highly appreciated
